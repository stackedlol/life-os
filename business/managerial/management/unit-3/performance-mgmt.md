### performance management
- is defined as a set or processes and managerial behaviors that involve defining, monitoring, measuring, evaluating, and providing consequences for performance expectations

### performance appraisals
- is a management process that consists of...
	1. assessing employee's performance
	2. providing them with feedback

- objective appraisals - are based on facts and are often numerical

- subjective appraisals - which are based on manager's perceptions of an employee's traits or behaviors 

- 360 degree feedback appraisal - employees are appraised not only by their managerial superiors, but also by peers, subordinates, and sometimes clients
