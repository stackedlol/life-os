### two types of change for organizations
1. reactive - making changes in response to problems or opportunities as they arise

2. proactive - involves making carefully thought-out changes in anticipation of possible or expected problems or opportunities

### demassification...

### Lewin's Model
- unfreezing - create the motivation to change
- changing - learn new ways of doing things
- refreezing - support & reinforce the change

### organizational development process
- diagnosis - what is the problem?
- intervention - what should we do to fix this?
- evaluation - how well has intervention worked?
- feedback - how can the diagnosis and intervention be further refined?
