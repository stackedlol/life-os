### management by objectives
- MBO is a four step process
	1. managers and employees **jointly set objectives** for the employee
		- performance objectives
		- behavioral objectives
		- learning objectives
	2. managers develop action plans
	3. managers and employees periodically review the employee's performance
	4. managers make a performance appraisal and rewards the employee according to results

### goals must "cascade"
- cascading goals - is the process of ensuring that the strategic goals set at the top level align or "cascade" downward with more specific short-term goals at lower levels
