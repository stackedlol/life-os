### variable costing income statements
- contrasts absorption costing, which is for external
- broken down into segmented income statements
	- a part or activity of an organization, such as...
		- divisions
		- individual sotres
		- geographical regions
		- customers
		- product lines

### what is variable costing?
- manufacturing costs that vary with output
- does not include any fixed manufacturing overhead cost

### what is absorption costing?
- treats all manufacturing as product costs (fixed and variable)
